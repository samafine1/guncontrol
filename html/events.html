PRAISE AND STEALING
This piece was inspired by Mike Bostock’s Visualizing Algorithms, David Mimno’s Understanding Machine Learning with D3, Bret Victor’s Media for Thinking the Unthinkable, and Setosa’s Explained Visually.7


All of the visualizations were created using the D3.js library. D3 is an exceptional piece of software, and presents a brilliantly low barrier to entry via an enormous amount of freely available online examples. If my last project was an exploration into ClojureScript, this one is an exploration into D3.8
